Oaxaca Loom Textiles: Blankets and Cushions

Our bedding collection is the epitome of cultural richness and local craftsmanship. We offer unique textiles that will transform your home with quality, artistry, and comfort. With the option to customize designs, our customers can co-create pieces that reflect their personal style, ensuring exclusivity and connection to Oaxacan tradition. Discover how these textiles can enrich your space with quality, beauty, and a piece of Mexico's cultural heritage.

Chiapas Textiles: Bed Feet and Cushions

Our handcrafted textiles from Chiapas are meticulously woven with the highest quality lamb's wool. These pieces are the result of a dedicated and meticulous artisanal process that captures the essence of local tradition and the exceptional skill of our master weavers. We offer you the unique opportunity to collaborate in the creative process, creating customized textiles that will resonate with your individuality and décor. Our Chiapas textiles offer a unique and beautiful experience. Each thread weaves a story of dedication, culture, and art.

Zapotec Textiles: Tapestries and Rugs

Our Zapotec textiles are a testament to the enduring beauty of traditional craftsmanship. Created on treadle looms using high-quality cotton, these textiles are a reflection of a rich and deep heritage. They are woven by skilled artisans who have inherited and continue to pass down techniques from generation to generation. We customize your design choice to create a piece that speaks directly to your spirit and style.

Yute rugs:

Our collection of natural jute rugs, hand-woven by artisans in India, represents the pinnacle of skill and dedication. Each rug is a unique piece, combining the elegant rusticity of jute with designs that evoke the rich Indian textile tradition. These rugs not only add a touch of warmth and texture to any space, but also celebrate the art of hand weaving, carrying with them stories of culture and dedication. These rugs lend your home an air of authenticity, natural beauty, and comfort.
